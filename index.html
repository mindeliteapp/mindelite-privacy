MindElite

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

MindElite is an AI-powered educational app designed to help students learn more effectively. Using your device's camera, students can scan textbook pages and worksheets to instantly generate personalized quizzes, practice worksheets, and vocabulary lists. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Camera images — photos of textbook pages you scan to generate quizzes
  • Photos from your library — images imported for quiz generation
  • Voice recordings — recorded during Teach It Back vocabulary practice
  • Study data — quiz scores, vocabulary, subjects — stored locally on your device
  • Email address — only if you sign in (Google or email OTP) for cloud sync

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Camera & photo images are sent to our AI service solely to generate quiz content. Images are not stored on our servers.
  • Voice recordings are processed entirely on-device by Apple's Speech Recognition — never sent to our servers.
  • Study data stays on your device unless you enable cloud sync.
  • Email is used only for account creation and sync — never for marketing.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — generates quizzes for Math & Science. Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini — generates quizzes for English, History & Vocabulary. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Workers — backend proxy. No user data stored. Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — optional cloud sync if you sign in. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Speech Recognition — on-device only, never leaves your device.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All study data is stored locally on your device using Apple SwiftData.
  • If you enable sync, data is encrypted in transit and stored securely on Supabase.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

6. Children's Privacy

  • MindElite is designed for students of all ages, including children under 13.
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what is needed for the app to function.
  • Parents can delete all data by deleting the app from the device.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data stays on your device until you delete the app.
  • Cloud sync data can be deleted via Profile → Delete Account.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: all data is visible within the app.
  • Delete: delete the app or use Profile → Delete Account.
  • Disable sync: toggle off in Profile settings anytime.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will revise the Effective Date when we do. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
